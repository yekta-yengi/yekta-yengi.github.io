SEM Image Classification

On nanoscience, nanoscale image classification done by eyes of scientists. Since it looks easy, it's a huge waste of time. On our project, we tried to improve this primitive process with AI.

With my team, we developed multiple models, using transfer learning methods, that can classify the Scanning Electron Microscope images to 9 classes with more than 95% success. We used ResNetV2 and InceptionV3 models with applying fine tuning. Most of the time, I was involved in the development of the model and the research of new methods.

We are still trying to improve. Check out the project and leave comments.
